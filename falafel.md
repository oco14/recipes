
# Falafel

### Ingreadients

- 16oz dry chickpeas
- 1 onion
- lotf of garlic
- parsley and cilantro
- spices
  - salt
  - pepper
  - cumin
  - chili powder or cayene pepper
- lemon juice
- flour
- baking soda

### Instructions
1. blend everytime together
2. fry or bake
